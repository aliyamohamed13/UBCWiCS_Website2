Platinum Sponsors

Elpha Secure is bringing together advanced cybersecurity technology and cyber insurance to equip small and midsize businesses with the same powerful tools that large enterprises use to defend and recover from cyberattacks. Our mission is to mount the most effective defense against cybercrime and its consequences, today and as digital threats continue to evolve. We combine end-user software with close analysis of telemetry data to ensure a robust, real-time response to sophisticated attacks, demystifying and streamlining the cybersecurity process for our users along the way.

Centre for Digital Media houses the Master of Digital Media program, a world-class, professional graduate program located in Vancouver, Canada, inspired by cutting-edge research and industry innovation. The Master of Digital Media (MDM) is Canada's first professional graduate program in digital media. This intensive program with internship engages students in real world projects and coursework that provides valuable leadership experience, training, and top industry connections.
