UBC Womxn in Computer Science

We aim to build a strong community of womxn in Computer Science at UBC. We seek to empower womxn by providing opportunities to build lasting connections with other womxn in tech, and supporting each other as we progress through our time at UBC!



Connect

Connect with other students who identify as women and are pursuing careers in technology at UBC!

Collaborate

Take advantage of opportunities to get to know and potentially work with other members on projects of shared interest!

Career

Gain exposure to career pathways in computer science, women interested in diverse fields and with different professional and scholarly experiences!
