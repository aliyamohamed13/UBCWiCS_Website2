2020-2021 Events

Axiom Zen Career Talk Event

April 16th

During this event members had an opportunity to learn about internship opportunities, the full time application process, company culture, Axiom Zen’s tech stack and get any questions they had answered by company HR and engineering representatives! Around 20 students attended this event.

WiCS x Splunk Event

April 7th

This event featured a short presentation from a Splunk recruiter to discuss the hiring process, followed by amazing software engineers speaking about their experiences and answering questions. Attendees had a chance to ask panelists any questions in breakout rooms about their career paths and more! There were 23 students in attendance for this event.

WiCS x SAP Recruiting Event

March 16th

During this event recruiters from SAP answered student questions and shared information about topics such as the SAP internship process, resume preparation, company culture, and more. There were 39 students in attendance.

WiCS x Fortinet Recruiting Event

March 11th

This was our second recruiting event with Fortinet! There were 15 students in attendance.

WiCS Arts and Crafts Destress Night

Feburary 15th

Members got together during the reading week to destress and work on felt needle kits or other creative projects together!

Technical Interview Workshop

Feburary 4th

Our second technical interview event of the year was a huge success! During this event, small groups worked to solve a typical technical interview question with a mentor and ask any questions they had about this portion of the interview process. There were 25+ students and 24 mentors in attendance at this event!

Meet WiCS Sponsor Company: Fortinet

Feburary 3rd

At this event, Fortinet’s recruiters and students came together to discuss Fortinet's services, hiring process and much more. More than 20+ students attended this event.

Career Planning Event

January 14th

This event was focused on helping WiCS students understand the variety of careers paths that can come from having a degree in computer science, engineering or other relevant majors. Students connect and network with industry mentors. There were 69 students and 11 mentors in attendance at this event!

WiCS Holiday Party Event

December 22nd

UBC WiCS members gathered for some post-finals fun and relaxation at this event! Smaller groups played games like Among Us and pictionary, and also created some fun seasonal art all together!

WiCS-ters Meet and Greet

November 26th

This joint event with SFU WiCS took place with the purpose of fostering a community where undergraduate and graduate students can network, seek career advice, and share life stories in a safe space. The event featured speed networking with icebreaker questions and a brainstorming session for planning career, research and social oriented events for the future.


Resume Review and Behavioral Workshop

November 10th

The focus of this event was to help women* in Computer Science students at UBC to prepare for resume and recruiter screening rounds of interviews. Students were matched up with an industry mentor to review their resume with and practice answering some behavioral interview questions! There were 8 mentors and 20 students at this event!


Virtual Halloween Party

October 30th

This social event featured a spooky virtual scavenger and competitive costume contest!


Technical Interview Workshop

October 21st

Being comfortable with technical interview takes practice, practice, practice! At this mock interview event, UBC WiCS members had the chance to interact with mentors from various companies and learn about their interviewing experiences. They were paired with one paired with one industry mentor and possibly one other student and provided with a typical interview question to discuss and solve. We had 27 attendees at this event!


Exec Ask Me Anything

September 16th, 2020

This was our first official social event of the year! UBC WiCS members had the chance to ask execs ANYTHING they wanted, topics discussed included co-op applications, course planning, career development, personal project, mentoring and hackathons!


Imagine Day

September 8th, 2020

Although a wildly different year than last, we kicked off the year with enthusiasm and excitedness at our virtual Imagine Day booth and engaged with women* UBC students from different years!
